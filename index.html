COVID-19 Update

In light of the impact that COVID-19 is having around the world, we are calling our global body to pray that Lord will shine bright through this confusing and unnerving time.

As a movement we encourage everyone to consult with their local and national health organizations to find current and correct information on what they can do. All of our churches will do our best to continue to meet the needs of their local congregations, and if able to provide a digital experience to continue our mission to help the world encounter Gods transforming presence!
